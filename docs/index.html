HVTN SDMC Reproducible Science Core

Advancing Transparent and Reproducible Statistical Science across HVTN Studies

Our Mission

The Reproducible Science Core strengthens the HIV Vaccine Trials Network's (HVTN) Statistical and Data Management Center (SDMC) by developing and promoting standardized, reproducible analytical workflows. We establish clear expectations for reproducibility in statistical analyses and manuscripts, maintain robust software tools, provide training and consultation, and foster adoption of state-of-the-art reproducible science practices. These principles extend beyond HVTN studies to external collaborative research efforts.
